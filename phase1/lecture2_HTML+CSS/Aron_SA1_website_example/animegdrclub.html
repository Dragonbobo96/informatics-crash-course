As seen in the homepage, Anime/GDR Club! is born on 28 march 2010 and it was an italian fan-site about animes and mangas and with a section dedicated to a written role-play game.
In 2013, we started a mangascan project, which was about translating mangas from english to italian (which were obviously inedited in italian). With the passing of the years, the group became bigger and bigger and we had the chance to translate over 60 different series.
If you're interested on knowing more about the translation work itself, you can find all the informations (in italian, obviously) on this topic of the site.
For what concerns me, I translated a small number of series and edited none of them, but I'm one of the main quality-checkers, having checked almost a half of all the series.
